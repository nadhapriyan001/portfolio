About Me

Building the Future

I'm M.S. Nadhapriyan, a multidisciplinary engineer passionate about merging hardware innovation with intelligent software. From designing AI-driven autonomous vehicles to building brain-computer interfaces, I thrive at the intersection of electronics, embedded systems, and cutting-edge software.

As the Founder of CLUX, a startup in R&D and Manufacturing, I lead a team of 10 in developing real-world solutions. My work has been recognized at national-level competitions including Smart India Hackathon and the IDEX Defence Innovation Challenge.

I hold two patents for my Cargo Cum Commute Electric Cycle — a dual-purpose E-cycle for sustainable transport. I'm proficient in Python, C, C++, Verilog, MATLAB and work extensively with embedded platforms like Arduino, ESP32, and Raspberry Pi.
